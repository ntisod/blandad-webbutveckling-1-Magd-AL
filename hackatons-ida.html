About

Our group name is NTI-Södertälje and we are working on an app/website that helps people who do not have access to schools or cheap education learn the english pronounciation.

Goal

Our goal is to help everyone that doesn't have a high quality education and to expand their language knowledge.

Future expectations

We are planing to expand our language library to over 100 languages. We will keep updating PronLearner and make it easy to access
